Welcome to Grab N Go ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account or use our services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, password, age, and gender
  • Profile Information: Profile picture (optional)
  • Payment Information: Payment card details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store your full card details)

1.2 Location Information

  • Real-time GPS Location: Used to display your location on the map, show nearby stores, and determine store zone availability
  • Location Tracking Frequency: Updated every 1.5 seconds or when you move more than 1 meter
  • Purpose: Enable express pickup services, show store locations, and verify service zones

You can disable location services through your device settings, but this may limit certain features.

1.3 Transaction and Order Information

  • Order history and details
  • Cart contents and shopping patterns
  • Favorite products and preferences
  • Order status and tracking information
  • Payment amounts and transaction records

1.4 Device and Technical Information

  • Device identifiers
  • Camera access data (barcode/QR scanning)
  • App usage statistics
  • Operating system and version
  • IP address

1.5 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Name and email address
  • Profile picture (if available)
  • Publicly available profile information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Providing Our Services

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process orders and payments
  • Enable self-checkout and express pickup
  • Verify store zone availability
  • Send order confirmations and updates

2.2 Communication

  • Transaction notifications
  • Customer support
  • Service-related updates

2.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and performance issues
